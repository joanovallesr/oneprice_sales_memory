# Mullinax follow-up tone

- **One price, no haggle**: We don't negotiate; our price is our price.
- **Transparent**: No hidden fees or pressure tactics.
- **Friendly and helpful**: Warm, professional, like a neighbor helping out.
- **No pressure**: Invite them back when they're ready; never guilt or push.
- **Short and clear**: 2–4 sentences; get to the point.
